Introduction to generative artificial intelligence software

❀ What is GAI?

This AI model generates new data with similar characteristics by learning the patterns and structures of the input training data. Improvements in Transformer-based deep neural networks, especially large language models (LLMs), fueled the development of generative AI systems in the early 2020s.

AI Image Generation Services

❀ Introduction

Image generate AI is a type of generative AI, where images can be returned by simply typing descriptive words or scenarios. In current trend, there is 2 main ways to train AI producing images.

First one being “Generative Adversarial Networks”, which trains two AI in the same time, one being the generator producing images with what they have already learned, while the other AI detects whether the produced images are generated or not. After millions of loops, both AIs will learn how to create images that are hard to determine its authenticity and how to define generated and real images respectively.

The second is “Variational Autoencoders”, it encodes input data into smaller information called “latent space”, then creates random points with the information of the latent space and inputted data to create images. The system will detect if the output matches with the original input (descriptive text we entered), and penalize the AI if it does not, in order to make it more accurate.

Both ways rely heavily on data feed into their training model, which can greatly affect the generated images’ style, however it may also lead to copyright problems which is discussed a lot recently.

There is currently multiple ways of telling the AI what image to create, such as text to image, image to image and fuse multiple images to one image.

AI Video Generation Services

❀ Introduction

Generative AI text-to-video technology is mainly based on deep learning and natural language processing.

First, we need a large dataset of text and video, where each text corresponds to a video. We then use a text-to-video generative AI model that consists of an encoder and a decoder.

The encoder converts the text input into a vector, and the decoder decodes that vector into the corresponding video.

In this way, we can turn any text into a corresponding video, even some scenes or objects we have never seen before. The application of generative AI is very broad.

In addition to converting simple text into video, it can also be applied to speech recognition, natural language processing, computer vision, and many other fields. In terms of video generation, one of the representatives of generative AI technology is GAN (Generative Adversarial Network).

Gans can use deep learning algorithms to convert simple text or pictures into realistic videos. For example, just enter a text description and a GAN can automatically generate a video corresponding to it. This technology is widely praised in the field of creative workers such as designers and artists and greatly improves creative efficiency.

In addition, generative AI technology can also create videos based on text prompts, which Silicon Valley investors are betting on, which can turn simple text into realistic images and even videos.

This technology's rise is changing how we think about content creation and information delivery.

Generative AI technology is mainly based on deep learning and natural language processing. Based on a given text input, these systems can be trained to understand natural language and generate images or videos with extremely high fidelity.

AI Voice Generation Services

❀ Introduction

The text-to-speech technology of generative AI is a deep learning-based technology that relies primarily on deep learning and neural networks. This technology can convert any input text into the corresponding speech. By learning a large amount of speech and text data, it can generate high-quality, realistic speech content, and achieve flexible and personalized speech output.

This technique uses an advanced deep learning architecture, such as a short term memory Network (LSTM) or a Feed-Forward Neural Network, to capture associations between text and speech. Through training, the neural network can learn the characteristics and rules of speech, so as to convert the input text into the corresponding speech.

TAt present, the mainstream AI text-to-speech technology is mainly divided into rule-based methods and deep learning-based methods. Rule-based approaches rely primarily on human-written rules and templates, while deep learning-based approaches train neural networks to generate speech through vast amounts of data.

In addition, in recent years, some new speech synthesis methods have been proposed, and the synthesis effect is relatively ideal. For example, the WaveNet synthesis system proposed by the DeepMind team, by building an autoregressive model, directly modeling the time domain sampling point, and obtaining high naturalness synthetic speech. On the other hand, the Char2Wav proposed by Yoshua Bengio's team and the Tacotron system proposed by Google directly establish the mapping relationship between input text and output speech, and realize end-to-end speech synthesis, and the synthesized speech effect can be close to the level of real people's pronunciation. These new methods are driving the development of AI text-to-speech technology.
